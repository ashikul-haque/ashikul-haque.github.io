My research is centered on resilient and secure LPWAN communication under jamming and coexistence. I focus on building systems and validating them through controlled experiments and reproducible evaluation.

Selected Topics
  • Cross-technology communication: signal-level interoperability between LR-FHSS and LoRa, including receiver-side detection and correction.
  • Learning-based coexistence: learning-based coordination mechanisms for dense multi-network environments.
  • Jamming defense: gateway-side recovery and multi-gateway diversity against reactive and collaborative jammers.
  • LPWAN MAC/PHY design: protocol mechanisms with predictable reliability and latency under realistic constraints.
