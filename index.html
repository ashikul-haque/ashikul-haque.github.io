Md Ashikul Haque

I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science at the University of Texas at Dallas, advised by Prof. Abusayeed Saifullah. My research focuses on resilient and secure low-power wide-area networks (LPWAN), including jamming defense, coexistence management, and signal-level cross-technology communication.

Wireless network security LPWAN (LoRa, LR-FHSS) IoT systems Learning-based optimization

Highlights

  • ACM/IEEE SenSys 2026 (accepted): signal-level cross-technology communication from LR-FHSS to LoRa.
  • IEEE INFOCOM 2025: deep reinforcement learning for coexistence management in LPWAN.
  • IEEE/ACM MobiHoc 2025 & ACM/IEEE IoTDI 2024: robustness and security against jamming in LoRa networks.
  • IEEE RTSS 2024: Burst-MAC for burst traffic in LoRa networks.

Research interests

  • Wireless network security
  • Machine learning for embedded systems
  • Internet of Things
