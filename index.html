Md Ashikul Haque is a PhD candidate in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Texas at Dallas, advised by Prof. Abusayeed Saifullah. He received MS in Computer Science from Wayne State Unviersity. His research interests include Internet-of-Things, Wireless Networks, and Machine Learning. His work has been published in top-notch conferences including ACM/IEEE SenSys, IEEE INFOCOM, IEEE RTSS, ACM MobiHoc, ACM/IEEE IoTDI, and ACM EWSN.

Research Interest

The overarching goal of Ashikul's research is to make communication for low-power IoT devices more resilient, robust, and secure while minimizing the load on end devices. He builds and evaluates end-to-end systems spanning PHY/MAC mechanisms, signal-level decoding pipelines, and learning-based coordination, validated through SDR testbeds and simulation.
